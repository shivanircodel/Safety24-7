SELF DEFENCE
FOR WOMEN

Self defense is something that has become a crucial must learn for all women. When we come across an attacker some key vulnerability spots to target in order to escape are the ears,eyes,throat,nose,knees,chest,groin and ankles.


DEFENSE TECHNIQUES

1. Wrist catch & finger pull

One method to avoid harasment or an attack is by catching the wrist of your attacker. When your attacker is getting handsy then this is one of the best ways to get him on the ground

With one hand grab the ring and little finger, with the other grab the middle and index fingers and pull both hands in opposite directions while bending the wrist away from yourself

When it isnt possible to catch the wrist of your attacker then another way to temporarily disorient him is by punching his adams apple or by hiting him with you finger at his adams apple and colar bones.

3. Front Grab

Then using your forehead hit the attackers nose. This will make him pull away from you, giving you the chance to hit him in the groin with your knee.

Incase there is still some distance between you and the attacker then you can further disable the attacker by straightening your left arm and using you right arm hiiting the attacker in his chin and nose with your palm and then kicking him in the groin.

4. Side Grab

If your attacker approaches you from your side then using an arched move you can hit the attackers jaw,temple or nose. This will cause the attacker to go back a few steps allowing you room to ellbow him in the belly or chest.

This will disorient the attcker and buy you some time to escape.

4. Side Grab

In the scenario that your attacker grabs you from behind quickly bend back and try to hit the attcker with the back of your head. The objective is to get the attacker to move his leg forward. Once he does quickly bend down and pull it up. This will cause him to lose balance and fall down.

5. Pushed aginst the wall

If your attacker pushes you against the wall then in this situation you should hit whatever vulnerable area you are able to reach. If the attacker has his hands above your neck then the armpit is an easier target. Else if he has his hands lower you can reach for the chest,neck or jaw areas. The most affective way however is hitting him with your head. Squat a little and then jump up and hit him in the jaw with your head.
